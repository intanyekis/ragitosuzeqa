Hoang Kieu’s Rise to Success

Hoang Kieu’s journey from Vietnam to the United States is a testament to his determination and perseverance. Born in Vietnam, Kieu and his family immigrated to the United States in 1975, seeking better opportunities and a brighter future. This move would set the stage for Kieu’s exceptional achievements in the business world.

After arriving in the United States, Kieu wasted no time in pursuing his dreams. He began his career at Abbott Laboratories, where he gained invaluable experience and honed his skills. Recognizing his potential, Kieu set out on a path to entrepreneurship, driven by a desire to make a lasting impact and create opportunities for others.

In 1985, Kieu founded Rare Antibody Antigen Supply (RAAS), a blood plasma company that would become a major player in the industry. This venture marked the beginning of Kieu’s remarkable success story. With his visionary leadership and entrepreneurial spirit, Kieu transformed RAAS into a leading provider of blood plasma products, revolutionizing the pharmaceutical industry.

Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to improving lives, Kieu continued to expand his presence in the business world. He partnered with the Shanghai Blood Center to establish Shanghai RAAS Blood Products, a company listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange. This collaboration further solidified Kieu’s position as a prominent figure in the pharmaceutical industry.

“I firmly believe in the power of dedication and hard work. My journey is a testament to the possibilities that arise when you pursue your dreams relentlessly.”

Kieu’s achievements have not gone unnoticed. In 2014, Forbes recognized his exceptional success and listed him as one of the richest Americans. This acknowledgment further validated Kieu’s relentless pursuit of excellence and solidified his position as a distinguished business figure.

Throughout his career, Kieu’s unwavering commitment to success and his ability to seize opportunities have propelled him forward. His story serves as an inspiration, demonstrating that with determination, resilience, and a vision, one can overcome obstacles and achieve greatness.
